📋  CALL DISPOSITIONS
DispositionWhen to Use
VOICEMAIL Call routes to voicemail, or you hear a recorded message even if it sounds like someone answering.
HUNG UP Seller intentionally ends the call. Seller is not interested and hangs up after your opening, or ends the call after Rebuttal 1.
NO ANSWER Long beep, continuous ringing, "tototot" sound, dead air, or someone accidentally picks up but does not respond.
QUALIFIED LEAD ✅
  • All qualifying questions asked.
  • Seller is willing and motivated to sell.
  • Property is vacant land (see exceptions below).
  • Property is NOT landlocked (has road access or easement).
  • Seller agreed to receive the property report and be transferred to Maya.

Acceptable structures (do NOT disqualify): Barns, mobile homes, RV hookups, cabins — not included in sale price.
Large property with house: Ask if seller is open to selling the remaining vacant portion.
NOT QUALIFIED ❌
  • Property has a house/building (unless large lot — ask about remaining vacant land).
  • Property is landlocked with no easements.
  • Property is listed with a realtor (EXCEPT: EAW, SAM, JAZ LAND).
  • Owner or decision-maker is a realtor.
  • Commercial land (EXCEPT: JAZ LAND).
  • WILLOW CAPITAL: Any property with a house, regardless of size.
NOT INTERESTED Seller is not interested after Rebuttals 1 and 2. If seller gives a firm "NO" after the first rebuttal → mark as Not Interested. Do not push further. Limit rebuttals to 1–2 attempts.
DO NOT CALL 🚫
  • Seller requests to be removed from the list.
  • Extremely upset, cursing, or using profane language.
  • Threatens to report us (police/lawyers) → end politely, mark DNC.
  • Insists on knowing your exact office address → do NOT provide it; end call and mark DNC.
  • Does not want callbacks.
⚠️ This lead will be removed from the queue.
WRONG NUMBER Person has NO connection to the name on file AND no vacant land in the same state.
⚠️ Do NOT mark wrong number immediately — follow the step-by-step guide below first!
⚠️ This lead will be removed from the queue.
CALLBACK
  • Same day: Take ownership — seller should not repeat the conversation.
  • Next day: Let dialer call; just set a time for the following day.
NOT AVAILABLE Seller is not available; someone else answered and informed you the seller is busy.
LIVE TRANSFER LEAD Used after you successfully transfer the call to Maya. ⚠️ Will be removed from queue.
NOT IN SERVICE You hear: "This phone number is not in service." ⚠️ Will be removed from queue.
INTERESTED BUYER Use this disposition when the person is not interested in selling — they want to BUY more land instead.

📞 Script for Interested Buyer:
  1. "Sure! Is this the best number to call you back?" (mention the highlighted phone number on file)
  2. "When will be the best time to call you back — morning, afternoon, or do you have any specific time?"
  3. "Just to confirm, your full name is [mention full name on file]?"
    → If no name on file: "Can I have your name please so that we can address you properly?"
⚠️ REMINDER: If you see DNC, Live Transfer, Qualified Lead, Wrong Number, Not In Service, or Not Qualified in the contact history — take a screenshot while on the call. These numbers should already be removed from the queue. Dispositions that may still be re-routed: Not Interested (after ~1 month), Hung Up, Voicemail.
🔄  WRONG NUMBER — STEP-BY-STEP HANDLING GUIDE
⚠️ Key Rule: Do NOT drop the call too early. Always look for another angle to convert — this helps generate leads without wasting data.
Updated: Apr 30, 2026
1

Person says it's a wrong number / they're not the name on file

First, acknowledge: "I apologize for the call."

Then ask: "Are you related to [name on file]?"

  • → YES: Proceed accordingly (treat as the right party).
  • → NO: Do NOT end the call yet. Move to Step 2.
2

Ask: Do they own the property on file?

"Do you own the property on file at [address]?"

  • → YES: Proceed with the script as normal. Before transferring to Maya, confirm their correct name.
  • → NO: Move to Step 3.
3

Ask about other vacant land (soft phrasing)

Ask: "Do you happen to have any vacant land?"

🚫 Do NOT say "Do you have vacant land you want to sell?" — this limits your chances by implying they must already want to sell.
  • → YES: Ask for the location, explain the purpose of the call, proceed with the script.
  • → NOT INTERESTED: Move to Step 4.
4

Seller has land but doesn't want to sell → Use rebuttals

Apply your rebuttal skills. Limit rebuttals to 1–2 attempts.

  • → CONVERTED: Confirm correct address, acreage, and seller's name before transferring to Maya. Move to Step 5.
  • → STILL NOT INTERESTED: Disposition as "Not Interested."
  • → TRULY NO CONNECTION + NO LAND: Disposition as "Wrong Number."
5

If converted — document correctly ✅

  • Get their correct name.
  • Update the CC form with the actual person you spoke with.
  • Note in documentation: "On the dialer it's [original name], but spoke to [actual name] who confirmed they own vacant land."
  • Confirm correct address, acreage, and name before transferring to Maya.
Key Reminders:
  • Always acknowledge first, THEN ask about the property.
  • Use "Do you happen to have any vacant land?" — softer phrasing opens the conversation better.
  • Always confirm correct name, address, and acreage before transferring to Maya.
  • If they truly have no connection and no land → mark as Wrong Number.

✅  QUALIFICATION STANDARDS — WHAT AGENTS CHECK
These are the qualification checks that fall under the agent's responsibility. Data-team items (estimated value, duplicate parcels, ownership duration) are NOT your responsibility.
CheckHow to Handle
1. Is the property vacant land? Required: Property must be vacant land — no houses or buildings.

Exceptions (may still qualify):
  • Large property with a house → Ask if seller is open to selling the remaining vacant portion.
  • Barns, mobile homes, RV hookups, cabins → Acceptable (not in sale price).
  • SAM → Can proceed even with a house.
  • Seacoast Land Partners → Also interested in residential with house on 20+ acres.
  • WILLOW CAPITAL → Disqualify any property with a house, regardless of size.
2. Is there a minimum acreage requirement? General rule: No minimum acreage.

Exceptions: SEAGRAPE (5+), UNPAVED (10+), KING LAND (5+), Seacoast Land Partners (1+ vacant).
3. Is the property landlocked? Landlocked = NOT accepted. Must have road access or easement.

Ask: "Do you have an easement?" → YES: Proceed. → NO: Not qualified. → UNSURE: Proceed.
4. Is the property listed with a realtor? General rule: Not accepted.
Exceptions: SAM, EAW LAND, JAZ LAND.
5. Are you speaking with the right person? Must be the landowner or someone directly involved in the decision.
Assistants, secretaries, managers → NOT acceptable. If not confirmed → callback before submitting.

🗣️  APPROVED REBUTTALS
🚫 MAXIMUM 2 REBUTTALS ONLY. If the seller says NO after Rebuttal 2 → mark as Not Interested immediately. Do NOT attempt a 3rd rebuttal. Do NOT push the seller into transferring — this is considered Force Transfer / Hard Selling and is an Auto-Fail.
😐 "I'm Not Interested"
R1

1st Rebuttal — Add value to the call

Option A (Latest approved):

"Totally understand. What we can do to add some value to the time you've spent on this call is provide you with a free updated property report for your land. This is completely free, and I promise it won't take much of your time. Just hear me out, okay? And if your land might be a fit, our founder will reach out."

Option B (Maria's approved):

"I understand. What we can do here first is to give you a free updated report about your land. This is just very quick — at least 1–2 minutes of your time, I promise. Just hear me out. Okay?"

Option C (Notion):

"Totally understand. Just so I'm clear — you wouldn't be interested in a free Property Report? It only takes a few minutes and shows things like zoning and access which most owners don't really know about."

R2

2nd Rebuttal — Curiosity pivot

Option A (Maria's approved):

"Totally understand — most of the owners we talk to say the same thing. Out of curiosity though, if you did decide to sell at some point, do you have any idea what the land might be worth right now?"

→ If seller says NO / doesn't know:

"That's actually why I called — we can generate a quick report with recent land sales around your property. It only takes about a minute."

Option B (Notion):

"I get it. Land owners feel that way — but the Land Report is precisely what our owners review before deciding whether to reach out with a cash offer. Even if we don't end up making one, you'd still walk away with clear info on your land."

🚫 STOP HERE. If seller is still not interested after R2 → say "I completely understand, I appreciate your time. Have a great day!" and mark as Not Interested. Do NOT do a 3rd rebuttal. Do NOT transfer without a clear YES — that is Force Transfer / Hard Selling = Auto-Fail.
💰 "What's Your Offer?" / "Seller Has a Price in Mind"
R1

Seller mentions they have a price in mind

Option A (Maria's approved):

"I completely understand that you have a price in mind, and that's totally fair. What we do first is make sure that number is realistic based on all the details of your property. We can give you a detailed report within 3 minutes about your land. We have a custom tool to help with that. This is just very quick — at least 1–2 minutes of your time. Just hear me out. Okay?"

Option B (Notion):

"I totally get why you'd ask that. We actually don't throw out random offers without checking the details first because we want to be fair. Maya just needs to verify things like access, zoning, and comps, and then we can see if your property is a potential fit."

R2

If seller still pushes for a number

"Totally understand — you want to know if it's worth your time. The reason we don't give an instant number is because the value depends on key details about the land. Maya will ask a few quick questions and build a Property Report. It only takes a few minutes."

If they still push: "We don't want to throw out a lowball number without knowing the facts. This way, you get something realistic, not random. Is it okay to transfer you over? Right?"

🚫 AUTO-FAIL: Never give an actual price or imply a cash offer amount.
